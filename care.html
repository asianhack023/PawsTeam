Caring Tips

For dogs:

Exercise:

Dogs need regular exercise. Start with short walks and gradually increase as they adjust to their new home.

Grooming:

Dogs need to be bathed every 4–6 weeks with appropriate shampoo. If possible, trim their nails every 3–4 weeks and brush their teeth a few times a week.

Socialization:

them to new experiences, people, and other animals gradually to help them become well- adjusted.



For cats:

Litter box setup:

up a litter box with suitable filling, like cat litter or newspaper scraps, and change it frequently to prevent your cat from defecating outside the box.

Scratching post:

Provide scratching posts or pads to help prevent damage to furniture.

Grooming:

Cats don't need much grooming because they self-groom. However, if something sticks to them, you may need to bathe them. Trim their nails every 2-3 weeks.



For both:

Visit the vet:

Schedule a check-up to ensure they are healthy and up-to-date on vaccination.

Nutrition:

Cats and dogs can eat rice and vegetables. Additionally, they love chicken heads and legs, and it is economical and convenient since it costs 50 npr per kilo.

Grooming:

While grooming, take the opportunity to check for any unusual signs such as lumps, bumps, or changes in skin condition. Look out for signs of parasites like fleas or ticks. If you notice anything concerning, consult your vet.
